About Us

Founded in 1987, Power of Jesus International Ministries is dedicated to spreading the gospel and serving the community in Kisii Central, Kenya. Our mission is to foster spiritual growth and provide a welcoming space for worship. Led by Bishop Joshua Anari Mokaya and First Lady Margret Ongeri Mokaya, we aim to inspire faith and compassion in all we do.
