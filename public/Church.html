Welcome to Power of Jesus International Ministries

Located in Kisii Central, Kenya, Power of Jesus International Ministries is a vibrant faith community committed to spreading the Gospel, nurturing discipleship, and transforming lives. Join us as we worship, grow in faith, and serve others with love and compassion.

About Us

Founded in 2000, Power of Jesus International Ministries exists to fulfill the Great Commission. With a heart for evangelism, discipleship, healing, and social transformation, we serve with purpose under the guidance of the Holy Spirit. We are a rising army of believers—young and old—called and anointed to be like the apostles and prophets of the Bible. We believe miracles, signs, and wonders are not history but a present-day reality through the Holy Spirit.

Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all people, equipping believers for spiritual maturity and serving communities with compassion.

  • To raise and equip a generation of righteous warriors.
  • To live by the revelatory Word of God with Holy Spirit manifestation.
  • To restore the fear of God and divine encounters.

Vision

A world transformed by the love and power of Jesus Christ. To ignite a global revival of righteousness, holiness, and supernatural power through Spirit-filled youth. (Acts 2:17 / Joel 2:28)

Core Values

  • Faith in Jesus
  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Service and Compassion
  • Unity in the Body of Christ
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Peace and Reconciliation

Church Goals

  • Evangelize and disciple youth with uncompromised teachings (Matthew 28:19)
  • Reveal hidden wisdom through prayer and fasting (Psalm 25:14)
  • Demonstrate power through healing, prophecy, and miracles (Mark 16:17–18)
  • Baptize in water and Holy Spirit (Acts 2:38)
  • Speak in tongues as evidence of Holy Spirit baptism (Acts 2:17)

Objectives

  1. Establish homes and hospitals for the elderly.
  2. Open counseling and training centers.
  3. Host national and international spiritual meetings.
  4. Set up rehabilitation and vocational programs.
  5. Promote unity and conduct spiritual ceremonies.

Donations / Giving

We warmly welcome well-wishers, partners, and volunteers from across the globe to join us in this divine mission of building the Body of Christ and extending compassion to the vulnerable communities served by Power of Jesus International Ministries.

Your generous donations, whether one-time gifts, recurring contributions, or grants, directly support our ministry's mission work, outreach programs, community empowerment, and charitable initiatives. With your help, we are able to spread the gospel, uplift the needy, provide spiritual and material support, and strengthen the faith of believers around the world.

No matter where you are in the world, your support makes a difference. Together, we can touch lives, restore hope, and build a stronger, faith-filled society rooted in love and truth.
